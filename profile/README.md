## 🏛️ About Us

The **Department of Sports — Southern Province (DPSP)** is the official provincial government body responsible for the development and administration of sports, youth affairs, rural development, cultural and art affairs, social welfare, probation & child care services, and women's affairs across the Southern Province of Sri Lanka.

Our **DevOps team** (`DPSP-DevOps`) drives the digital transformation of provincial government services — building scalable, secure, and modern web applications to bring efficient governance to citizens and communities.

> 📍 **Address:** 3rd Floor, Dakshinapaya, Ministry Complex, Labuduwa, Galle, Sri Lanka

## 📁 Repository Guidelines

We follow consistent practices across all repositories:

- **Branch naming:** `feature/`, `fix/`, `hotfix/`, `chore/`
- **Commit style:** Follow [Conventional Commits](https://www.conventionalcommits.org/)
- **Code review:** All PRs require at least one reviewer before merging
- **Secrets:** Never commit credentials or API keys — always use `.env` files
- **Documentation:** Keep each project's `README.md` up to date
